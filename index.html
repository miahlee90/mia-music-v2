Just need one topic? Open the lesson index, search your topic, review it, practice, done.

How each lesson works

Every lesson walks the same path, one card at a time:

🎯 Today's Mission — what you'll be able to do by the end. · 🃏 Vocabulary — flip-cards for the new terms and symbols. · 🎹 Learn by Doing — press, play, and answer as Mia explains each idea. · 🎵 See and Hear It in Examples — the concept inside real musical examples. · 💡 Remember! + Mia's Tips — a quick recap and the mistakes to watch for. · ✏️ Practice — one question at a time with instant feedback. · 🎮 Mini-Game — play with the concept, just for fun. · ✅ Final Quiz — fresh questions every attempt.

Sections open as you go, but nothing is locked — skip ahead or repeat anything, and retake the Final Quiz as many times as you like. Only the quiz counts: when you're signed in with your class codes, your Final Quiz score and lesson completion are reported to your instructor automatically.
